About SmartChef AI

SmartChef AI is on a mission to transform your everyday ingredients into delightful meals. We believe that cooking at home should be smart, easy, and delicious.

Our Vision

To empower every home cook with AI that eliminates food waste and simplifies mealtime decisions—making healthy eating more accessible and enjoyable.

Our Technology

SmartChef AI combines intelligent recipe generation with user preferences to deliver meals tailored to you. Our engine learns and adapts to your pantry and tastes.

Our Team

We're a passionate team of food lovers, engineers, nutritionists, and creators building the future of personalized cooking, one meal at a time.

Why We Built SmartChef

We were tired of staring into our fridges and asking, “What can I make with this?” So we built SmartChef AI to solve that problem—and we’re just getting started.
